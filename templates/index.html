Efficient waste management reduces environmental damage, saves energy, and promotes a cleaner future. Proper segregation improves recycling outcomes and lowers landfill burden.

  • Organic: Biodegradable; great for composting.

Did You Know?

  • Recycling 1 plastic bottle saves enough energy to power a light bulb for 3 hours.
  • Organic waste makes up over 50% of household garbage and can be composted.
  • Aluminum cans are 100% recyclable and can be reused endlessly.
  • Plastic takes over 400 years to break down in landfills.

Impact of Proper Waste Segregation

  • 1 ton of recycled paper saves 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water.
  • Recycling metal saves up to 95% of the energy used for producing new metal.
  • Proper sorting reduces landfill use by over 60%.
