Reuse Suggestions for {{ waste_type }}

Here are some creative and eco-friendly ways to reuse your {{ waste_type }} waste:

{% if waste_type == 'plastic' %}

Plastic Facts

  • Plastic is a non-biodegradable material.
  • It belongs to the dry waste category.
  • Reusing plastic helps reduce marine pollution.
  • It can be turned into eco-bricks, planters, or craft items.
{% elif waste_type == 'paper' %}

Paper Facts

  • Paper is dry waste and is biodegradable.
  • It can be recycled up to 6 times.
  • Reused as packaging, craft paper, or compost.
{% elif waste_type == 'glass' %}

Glass Facts

  • Glass is 100% recyclable without loss of quality.
  • It is considered dry waste.
  • Can be reused as containers, decor, or mosaics.
{% elif waste_type == 'metal' %}

Metal Facts

  • Metals are dry waste and highly recyclable.
  • Commonly reused as scrap or for DIY crafts.
  • Recycling metal saves natural ore and energy.
{% elif waste_type == 'organic' %}

Organic Waste Facts

  • Organic waste is biodegradable and wet waste.
  • Includes food scraps, leaves, peels, etc.
  • Perfect for composting and enriching soil.
{% endif %}
